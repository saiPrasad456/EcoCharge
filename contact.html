Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find charging stations?

Use our mobile app or website station locator to find the nearest EcoCharge stations. You can filter by charger type, availability, and pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and our EcoCharge membership cards.

How long does it take to charge my EV?

Charging time depends on your vehicle and charger type. Level 2 chargers typically take 4-8 hours, while DC Fast chargers can charge to 80% in 20-40 minutes.

Do you offer home installation?

Yes! We provide professional home installation services for Level 2 chargers, including site assessment, permits, and warranty coverage.

What if a charging station is broken?

Report any issues through our mobile app or call our 24/7 support line. We typically resolve technical issues within 4 hours.

Can I reserve a charging station?

Yes, EcoCharge Plus members can reserve charging stations up to 24 hours in advance through our mobile app.
